About me

Hello,
I'm Lupo

Sales operator by trade. AI builder by obsession. Somewhere in between is the most interesting place to be standing right now.

L // lupo

I spent a decade carrying a bag at places like Salesforce, Intuit, and Yext — learning how to communicate value to people who'd heard every pitch before. Then in 2023 I started using AI for real, every day, and the ground shifted under me.

I'm not a developer. I don't deploy APIs or write Python from scratch. I orchestrate — I build systems where AI does the heavy lifting and I make the call on what's good enough to ship. Turns out the years of cutting through noise transfer surprisingly well.

Here's the honest tension: I have every skill to keep doing what I've always done. But I've seen enough to know that's not the move. So I'm building in the open instead — through Lupo Studios, a one-person AI learning and creative lab. This site is the diary of that.

I learned in public, got helped by strangers on the internet, and I pay it forward automatically. Not as a strategy. That's just how I'm wired.

How I think

A few things I keep coming back to

The AI Sandwich

Humans are the bread, agents are the filling. When everyone has the same tools, the competitive edge shifts to taste and acumen. I like that.

The Allocation Economy

Better questions beat better answers. You don't get paid for what you know anymore — you get paid for knowing where to point the machine.

Shake & Bake

Two modes. Move fast to find the value, then slow down and build it right. The mistake is trying to do both in the same breath.

Writing as Discovery

Writing forces clarity, and clarity generates the next idea. Half of what's on this site I didn't understand until I tried to explain it.
