03 — About The human

A sales operator who fell into the command line.

I spent a decade carrying a bag at places like Salesforce, Intuit, and Yext — learning to cut through noise for people who'd heard every pitch. In 2024 I started using AI for real, and the ground shifted.

I'm not a developer. I orchestrate — I build systems where AI does the heavy lifting and I make the call on what ships. This is the diary of figuring that out, in public.
